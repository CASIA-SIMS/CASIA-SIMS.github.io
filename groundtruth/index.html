Groundtruth 3D Point Clouds

The ground truth 3D point clouds are acquired simultaneously with the corresponding trajectories. We merge the 3D point clouds as a complete point cloud for each scene.


Groundtruth Trajectories

The timestamped translation vector and unit quaternion of a ground truth trajectory from the camera coordinate to the world coordinate are formatted as:

timestamp[i] p_x p_y p_z q_x q_y q_z q_w

Similarly, trajectories are transformed into a unified world coordinate system.
